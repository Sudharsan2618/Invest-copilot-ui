<KeyConcept title="The Uncomfortable Truth About Market Cycles">
  The best time to invest is when it FEELS the worst. And the worst time to
  invest is when it FEELS the best. By the time you feel confident about the
  market, most of the gains have already happened. By the time you feel scared,
  most of the losses have already happened. Your emotions are the worst
  investment advisor you have.
</KeyConcept>
