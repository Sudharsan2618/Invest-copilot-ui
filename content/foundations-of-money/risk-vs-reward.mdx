## Redefining Risk: The Word You've Been Misunderstanding

If there's one word in finance that's most misunderstood, it's "risk." Most people hear "risk" and think "danger" â€” the chance of losing everything. But in investing, risk means something very different: **volatility** â€” the ups and downs along the way.

Understanding this distinction is the difference between making smart investment decisions and letting fear keep you poor.

### Time Eliminates Risk

Here's the crucial nuance: risk decreases with TIME. The Nifty 50 might drop 30% in any given year. But over any 10-year period, it has always been positive. Over 15 years, it has always beaten FDs.

**Short-term volatility is the price you pay for long-term wealth.** If you're not going to need the money for 10+ years, equity is paradoxically the SAFEST investment because history guarantees it will outperform.

</ConceptImage>
