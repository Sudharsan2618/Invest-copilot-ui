Fixed Deposits. The national investment of India. Your parents have them. Your grandparents swore by them. Bank managers love selling them.

And for most people in the 30% tax bracket, FDs are **quietly destroying wealth** while making you feel safe.

Let's understand when FDs make sense, when they don't, and what the alternatives are.

<Analogy>
  An FD is like keeping food in the refrigerator. It slows down spoilage
  (inflation erosion). But it doesn't make the food grow or multiply. If you
  keep food in the fridge for 10 years, it's still going bad â€” just slower. For
  short periods, the fridge is great. For long periods, you need to plant a
  garden (equity investments) that actually produces new food.
</Analogy>
